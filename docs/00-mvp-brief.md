# Homelab Fit MVP Brief

## Site Concept

Build a fast, source-backed compatibility wiki for Proxmox homelab builders choosing mini PCs, NICs, storage layouts, and passthrough setups.

## Audience

- Mini PC NAS buyers
- Proxmox beginners who are about to install
- Homelab users comparing N100, N305, Ryzen, and workstation-style boxes
- Users planning ZFS, TrueNAS VM, Home Assistant VM, firewall VM, or small clusters

## Page Types

- Hardware fit pages
- Networking fit pages
- Storage fit pages
- Passthrough fit pages
- Comparison pages
- Buyer checklists
- Operations checklists
- Error fix pages

## First Success Metrics

- 80-120 source-backed pages live
- More than 70% indexed after sitemap submission
- At least 20 pages with impressions
- At least 5 pages with clicks
- At least one buying-intent cluster with repeatable impressions
