## Quality Gate

Promote a candidate only when it has:

- Primary source links
- Access date
- Updated date
- Applies-to context
- Four or more key facts
- Five or more recommended checks
- Three or more verification checks
- Warnings
- Fit and not-fit criteria
- Related page links

## Expansion Rule

Do not add a second niche until this project has:

- More than 70% of submitted pages indexed
- At least 20 pages with search impressions
- At least 5 pages with clicks
- At least one hardware or storage cluster showing repeatable query patterns
